About Haus + Home

For 3 weeks only, NORDEN and Dear Home are setting up shop in downtown Urbana—just in time for the holidays! Featuring modern and vintage furnishings, decor, and accessories for the home, stop by to find the perfect gift for your friends, your family, and yourself. New items added weekly!

NORDEN is an up-and-coming German design studio that designs and manufactures a line of simple, yet refined products for the home.
norden-design.com

Dear Home scouts out unique and stylish vintage goods, curating a collection that brings design-inspired objects from the past into your home.
dearhomevintage.com
